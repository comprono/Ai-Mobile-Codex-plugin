# AI Mobile v0.4.1 - Smart Compact Communication

AI Mobile now thinks deeply and communicates compactly by default.

- Answer and outcome first.
- No filler, repeated prompt, tool narration, waiting commentary, or routine postamble.
- Exact evidence, warnings, caveats, code, commands, paths, errors, and necessary reasoning remain intact.
- Automatic expansion for ambiguity, confusion, risk, irreversible actions, and important decisions.
- Private modes: `smart-compact`, `standard`, and `detailed`.

The feature changes communication cost, not intelligence or verification depth. It adds no new tool or background process.
