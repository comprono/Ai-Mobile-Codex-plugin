The CFO forecasts the complete active plan while allocating only the dependency-ready bundle. Every future package is recorded exactly once as explicitly deferred without influencing the model chosen for current work.

CFO allocation and final routing share provider cooldown state, so a failed provider is not immediately selected again. The Antigravity adapter imports its full locally measured model roster and places conservative per-invocation reservations against the chosen model pool before launch.

Intermediate discovery activity may receive deterministic evidence links, but final integration, end-to-end, and user-visible proof is never synthesized or upgraded.

Reconciliation accepts structured context-refresh directives and optional budget metadata when an independently actionable plan or contract correction exists. Budget-only narrative remains insufficient for retry.

The exact-state canary evaluates real Director outcomes rather than an exact round count and preserves its disposable state on failure. Installation remains prohibited until a fresh mutation-guarded Job Vibhu clone proves context integration, assured strategy, plan-wide budgeting, team compilation, exact executor ownership, the first safe execution route, and unchanged production state.
